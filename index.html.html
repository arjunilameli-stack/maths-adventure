Maths Adventure

A fun, step-by-step way to find your tricky spots and turn them into your superpowers!

Covers all 16 units of your Class 5 textbook, in 5 groups: Geometry, Arithmetic, Measurement, Statistics and Algebra. The Practice Arena cooks up a fresh set of at least 100 questions per unit, across 3 levels — so it's never quite the same twice!
Everything stays right here in this browser. Tap "Save / export as text" in Tab 5 before you close the tab, so your progress is never lost.

Part One · No pressure, no clock in her mind

Full Diagnostic Tracker — All 16 Units

Grouped into the textbook's 5 categories — Geometry, Arithmetic, Measurement, Statistics and Algebra. Do one unit per sitting, a few days apart. Tap a unit below to open it; the rest stay tucked away so it never feels like 31 questions at once. Ask each question out loud, watch how she gets to the answer (not just whether it's right), and mark what you notice.

🦉

Part Two · Make the number physical

Interactive Manipulatives

Sixteen hands-on tools, one for every unit in the textbook — grouped by Geometry, Arithmetic, Measurement, Statistics and Algebra, in unit order. Use these on screen together, or as a warm-up before homework. Let her drive the sliders and inputs herself.

Part Three · Teach the concept properly, once

Learn: Step-by-Step Tutorials

One tutorial per strand — a plain-language explanation, the rule to remember, and worked examples you can read together before she tries it herself. Use this right after a gap shows up in Tab 1. Reveal each example one step at a time — figure it out together before checking the next clue.

Part Four · Now she tries it

Practice: Exercises

Six questions per strand, easiest first. Let her work on paper, then tap "Show answer" to check — not before. Getting a few wrong here is useful information, not a failure.

Part Five · What we know now

Progress Summary & Action Plan

Combines the diagnostic tracker (Tab 1) with actual Practice Arena results (Tab 6) into one picture. Updates automatically — tap "Refresh report" any time. Export it as a text file to keep a dated record over time.
